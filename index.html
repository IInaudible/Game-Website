Controls

Click the green flag to start the game. To move the otter use the arrow keys, try to eat as many fish as you can without getting hit by plastic waste. You only have 3 lives so be careful!

About Our Game

Our game is about an otter swimming in the ocean and trying to eat fish while dodging waste pollution. We want our game to bring attention to the massive amount of plastic waste being thrown into oceans and what you can do to help prevent more plastic pollution.
