About Us

We are making this game as a poject to promote awareness for pollution and how it harms life below water.

About Our Game

Our game is about an otter swimming in the ocean and trying to eat fish while dodging waste pollution. The player will control the otter with the arrow keys, the score is based on how many fish are eaten and you have a set number of "hearts" which go down everytime you get hit by pollution.
